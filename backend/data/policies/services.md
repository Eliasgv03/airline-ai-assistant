# Air India In-Flight Services & Experience

**Source**: Verified Air India Policy Data (2024-2025)

## Dining Experience (The New Menu)
Air India provides complimentary meals on almost all flights (Domestic & International). The menu has been recently refreshed to offer a blend of Indian and International gourmet options.

### Meal Service by Cabin
*   **Economy Class**: Wholesome, hot meals with a choice of Veg or Non-Veg on meal-time flights. Refreshments on short sectors.
*   **Premium Economy**: Upgraded meal presentation, wider choice of beverages.
*   **Business Class**: Gourmet plated meals, "dine-on-demand" (select routes), premium wines, and spirits.
*   **First Class**: Exclusive multi-course ala carte dining, champagne service, and luxury table setting.

### Special Meals
Must be booked at least **48 hours** in advance via "Manage Booking". Common options include:
*   Asian Vegetarian (AVML)
*   Vegan (VGML)
*   Jain Meal (No onion/garlic)
*   Gluten-Free (GFML)
*   Child Meal (CHML) / Baby Meal (BBML)
*   Kosher (KSML)

## In-Flight Entertainment (Vista Stream)
Air India offers a modern entertainment experience, including the "Vista Stream" wireless streaming service on many narrow-body aircraft.

*   **Content Library**: Over 1,500 hours of movies (Bollywood, Hollywood, Regional), TV shows, and music.
*   **Access**:

## Cabin Features
*   **Economy**: Ergonomic seats with decent legroom. USB charging ports on newer aircraft.
*   **Business / First**: Lie-flat beds on international long haul (B777/B787/A350). Privacy dividers and direct aisle access on the new A350 fleet. Amenity kits by luxury brands (e.g., TUMI, Ferragamo) on long-haul flights.
