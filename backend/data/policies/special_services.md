# Air India Special Services and Assistance

**Source**: Verified Air India Policy Data (December 2024)

## Special Assistance for Passengers

### Passengers with Disabilities

#### Wheelchair Services

**Types of Wheelchair Assistance**:
- **WCHR (Wheelchair Ramp)**: Can walk short distances, needs wheelchair for long distances
- **WCHS (Wheelchair Stairs)**: Cannot climb stairs, can walk short distances
- **WCHC (Wheelchair Cabin)**: Completely immobile, needs wheelchair to/from seat

**How to Request**:
- At time of booking (recommended)
- At least 48 hours before departure
- Through airindia.com, call center, or travel agent
- **No additional charge** for wheelchair service

**Airport Assistance**:
- Dedicated staff at check-in
- Priority boarding
- Assistance through security
- Escort to aircraft
- Help with boarding/deplaning
- Assistance to baggage claim and exit

#### Mobility Aids and Equipment

**Allowed Items** (Free of charge):
- Manual wheelchairs
- Electric wheelchairs (with battery restrictions)
- Walkers and canes
- Crutches
- Prosthetic devices

**Battery-Powered Wheelchairs**:
- Lithium batteries: Must be removed and carried in cabin
- Wet cell batteries: Must be properly packaged
- Notify airline at least 48 hours in advance

#### Visual Impairment

**Services Provided**:
- Escort through airport
- Assistance with check-in and boarding
- In-flight assistance
- Guide dogs allowed in cabin (free of charge)

**Guide Dog Requirements**:
- Must be properly trained service animal
- Required documentation:
  - Vaccination certificates
  - Health certificate
  - Training certification
- Must be leashed and muzzled during flight
- Must fit at passenger's feet

#### Hearing Impairment

**Services Provided**:
- Visual announcements when possible
- Written communication at check-in
- Crew briefing for in-flight assistance
- Priority assistance during emergencies

### Medical Conditions

#### Passengers Requiring Medical Oxygen

**Requirements**:
- Medical certificate from doctor (issued within 7 days of travel)
- Advance notification (at least 48 hours)
- Air India provides POC (Portable Oxygen Concentrator)
- **Personal oxygen cylinders not allowed**

**Approval Process**:
1. Submit MEDIF (Medical Information Form)
2. Provide doctor's certificate
3. Air India medical team reviews
4. Approval typically within 24-48 hours

#### Stretcher Service

**Availability**:
- Available on select aircraft
- Requires booking of 6-9 seats (depending on aircraft)
- Must be requested at least 7 days in advance

**Requirements**:
- Medical certificate
- MEDIF form
- Accompanying medical attendant (mandatory)
- Advance payment for seats

**Cost**:
- Charged at business class fare × number of seats
- Medical attendant travels at applicable fare

#### Pregnant Passengers

**Travel Restrictions**:
- **Up to 32 weeks**: No restrictions (domestic)
- **Up to 36 weeks**: No restrictions (international)
- **32-36 weeks (domestic)**: Medical certificate required
- **After 36 weeks**: Not permitted to travel

**Medical Certificate Requirements** (if applicable):
- Issued within 7 days of travel
- Must state:
  - Expected delivery date
  - Fitness to fly
  - No complications
- Signed by registered medical practitioner

#### Passengers with Infectious Diseases

**General Policy**:
- Passengers with contagious diseases may be denied boarding
- Medical clearance required for:
  - Tuberculosis
  - Measles
  - Chickenpox
  - Other communicable diseases

**COVID-19 Specific** (subject to change):
- Follow current government guidelines
- May require vaccination proof or negative test
- Check destination country requirements

### Unaccompanied Minors (UM)

#### Age Requirements

**Domestic Flights**:
- **5-11 years**: Must use UM service (mandatory)
- **12-17 years**: UM service optional but recommended
- **Under 5 years**: Must be accompanied by adult (18+)

**International Flights**:
- **5-11 years**: Must use UM service (mandatory)
- **12-17 years**: UM service optional but recommended
- **Under 5 years**: Must be accompanied by adult (18+)

#### UM Service Includes

**At Departure**:
- Dedicated check-in counter
- UM tag and documentation
- Escort through security
- Supervision in waiting area
- Priority boarding
- Introduction to cabin crew

**During Flight**:
- Assigned crew member oversight
- Assistance with meals
- Entertainment and comfort items
- Regular check-ins

**At Arrival**:
- Escort off aircraft
- Assistance through immigration/customs
- Hand-over to authorized person only
- Verification of ID required

#### UM Service Fees

**Domestic**:
- INR 1,500 - INR 3,000 per child (each way)

**International**:
- INR 3,000 - INR 6,000 per child (each way)
- Varies by route

#### Booking UM Service

**How to Book**:
- Must be done at least 48 hours before departure
- Call Air India reservations
- Cannot be booked online
- Complete UM form

**Required Information**:
- Child's full name and age
- Contact details of person dropping off
- Contact details of person picking up
- Emergency contact information

**Drop-off/Pick-up Requirements**:
- Adult must remain at airport until flight departs
- Adult picking up must have:
  - Government-issued photo ID
  - Authorization letter (if not parent/guardian)
- ID verification mandatory

### Senior Citizens

#### Age Qualification
- **60 years and above** (domestic)
- **65 years and above** (international)

#### Benefits
- Priority check-in
- Priority boarding
- Wheelchair assistance (if requested)
- Extra assistance through airport
- Preferential seating (when available)

#### Discounts
- **Domestic**: Up to 50% discount on select fares
- **International**: Varies by route and season
- Must show proof of age at check-in

### Infants and Children

#### Age Categories
- **Infant**: Under 2 years (lap child)
- **Child**: 2-11 years

#### Infant Travel

**Lap Infant** (under 2 years):
- Travels on parent's lap
- Fare: 10% of adult fare (domestic), 10-25% (international)
- No separate seat
- One infant per adult

**Infant with Seat**:
- Can book separate seat for infant
- Must use approved car seat
- Charged at child fare
- Car seat must meet safety standards

#### Infant Amenities
- Bassinets available (request at booking)
  - For infants up to 11 kg
  - Limited availability
  - First-come, first-served
- Baby meals available (pre-order 24 hours in advance)
- Bottle warming service
- Changing tables in lavatories

#### Child Fare
- **Domestic**: 25-50% discount on adult fare
- **International**: 25-75% discount on adult fare
- Includes:
  - Separate seat
  - Full baggage allowance
  - Meal service
  - Entertainment

### Passengers Traveling with Pets

#### In-Cabin Pets

**Eligibility**:
- Small dogs and cats only
- Combined weight (pet + carrier) must not exceed 5 kg
- Carrier dimensions: Max 46 cm × 28 cm × 24 cm
- Must fit under seat in front

**Requirements**:
- Health certificate (issued within 10 days)
- Vaccination records (rabies, etc.)
- Import/export permits (international)
- Advance booking required (48 hours minimum)

**Fees**:
- **Domestic**: INR 2,000 - INR 3,000 per pet
- **International**: INR 5,000 - INR 10,000 per pet

**Restrictions**:
- Maximum 2 pets per flight
- Not allowed in Business/First class
- Must remain in carrier throughout flight

#### Checked Pets (Cargo Hold)

**Eligibility**:
- Larger dogs and cats
- Maximum weight: 32 kg (pet + carrier)
- Carrier must be IATA-approved

**Requirements**:
- Same documentation as in-cabin
- Sturdy, ventilated carrier
- Food and water containers
- Absorbent material in carrier
- Book at least 48 hours in advance

**Fees**:
- Charged as excess baggage
- Varies by weight and route
- Typically INR 200-400 per kg

**Restrictions**:
- Not accepted on certain routes
- Temperature restrictions apply
- Breed restrictions (snub-nosed breeds)

### Special Meal Requests

#### Available Special Meals

**Dietary Requirements**:
- Vegetarian (VGML)
- Vegan (VEGN)
- Hindu vegetarian (HNML)
- Jain vegetarian (VJML)
- Kosher (KSML)
- Halal (MOML)
- Diabetic (DBML)
- Low sodium (LSML)
- Gluten-free (GFML)
- Lactose-free (LFML)

**Religious/Cultural**:
- Hindu meal (HNML)
- Muslim meal (MOML)
- Kosher meal (KSML)

**Age-Specific**:
- Baby meal (BBML) - under 2 years
- Child meal (CHML) - 2-11 years

#### How to Request
- At time of booking (recommended)
- Through Manage Booking (up to 24 hours before)
- Call center (up to 24 hours before)
- **Not available** for last-minute requests

#### Important Notes
- Availability varies by route and class
- Some meals may not be available on short flights
- Confirm meal selection at check-in

### Group Travel

#### Group Booking Definition
- **10 or more passengers** traveling together
- Same flight and date
- Same origin and destination

#### Group Benefits
- Discounted fares
- Flexible payment terms
- Name changes allowed (up to departure)
- Dedicated group desk support
- Coordinated seating

#### How to Book
- Contact Air India Group Desk
- Email: groups@airindia.in
- Phone: +91 124 264 1407
- Provide:
  - Number of passengers
  - Travel dates
  - Route
  - Passenger details (can be added later)

### Sports Equipment

#### Accepted Sports Equipment

**Standard Items** (within baggage allowance):
- Golf clubs (1 set)
- Skis and poles (1 pair)
- Snowboard
- Bicycle (folded/boxed)
- Surfboard (under 2m)
- Diving equipment

**Packaging Requirements**:
- Must be properly packed
- Hard case or protective bag
- Remove or secure loose parts
- Deflate balls

**Fees**:
- If within baggage allowance: No extra fee
- If exceeds allowance: Excess baggage charges apply
- Oversized items: Additional handling fee

#### Advance Notification
- Recommended for:
  - Bicycles
  - Surfboards
  - Large equipment
- At least 48 hours before departure

### Musical Instruments

#### Cabin Carry-On
- Small instruments (violin, flute, etc.)
- Must fit in overhead bin or under seat
- Counts as cabin baggage

#### As Checked Baggage
- Larger instruments (guitar, etc.)
- Must be in hard case
- Subject to size/weight limits

#### Extra Seat Purchase
- For valuable/fragile instruments (cello, etc.)
- Purchase adjacent seat at applicable fare
- Instrument must be secured
- Maximum weight: 75 kg

### Excess Baggage

#### Pre-Purchase Online
- Available on airindia.com
- Up to 20% cheaper than airport rates
- Can be purchased up to 4 hours before departure

#### Airport Rates

**Domestic**:
- INR 600 per kg + GST

**International**:
- Varies by zone
- Typically INR 800-1,500 per kg
- USD 20-50 per kg on some routes

## Important Reminders

### Advance Notice
- Most special services require **48 hours advance notice**
- Medical clearances may take **48-72 hours**
- Group bookings should be made **at least 2 weeks** in advance

### Documentation
- Always carry required medical certificates
- Keep copies of all approvals
- Bring government-issued ID
- Have emergency contact information

### Airport Arrival
- Arrive **3 hours early** for international flights requiring special assistance
- Arrive **2 hours early** for domestic flights requiring special assistance
- Report to designated special assistance counter
