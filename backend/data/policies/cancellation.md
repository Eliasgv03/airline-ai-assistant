# Air India Cancellation Policy

**Source**: Verified Air India Policy Data (December 2024)

## 24-Hour Free Cancellation Rule

### Domestic and International Flights
- **Free cancellation** within 24 hours of booking
- **Condition**: Departure must be at least 7 days away
- **Refund**: Full refund without any cancellation charges
- **Applies to**: Tickets booked directly with Air India

## Cancellation Charges After 24 Hours

### Domestic Flights

#### By Fare Type
- **Value Fares**: 
  - Only taxes refunded
  - Base fare is non-refundable
  
- **Classic Fares**:
  - More than 72 hours before departure: INR 2,500
  - Within 72 hours of departure: INR 3,500
  
- **Flex Fares**:
  - More than 72 hours before departure: No fee
  - Within 72 hours of departure: Minimal fee applies

### International Flights

#### General Cancellation Fees
- **Standard penalty**: 20-30% of the fare
- **Fee range**: INR 3,000 to INR 7,500 depending on:
  - Cabin class (Economy, Premium Economy, Business, First)
  - Time before departure
  - Fare type (Value, Classic, Flex)

#### By Cabin Class
- **Economy Class**: 
  - More than 72 hours: ~20% of fare
  - Within 72 hours: ~30% of fare
  
- **Premium Economy**: 
  - More than 72 hours: ~15% of fare
  - Within 72 hours: ~25% of fare
  
- **Business/First Class**: 
  - More than 72 hours: ~10% of fare
  - Within 72 hours: ~20% of fare

## No-Show Policy

### What is a No-Show?
A no-show occurs when a passenger:
- Does not cancel the ticket before departure
- Misses the flight without prior notification

### No-Show Consequences
- **Base fare**: Typically not refunded
- **Taxes**: Only applicable airport taxes may be returned
- **Depends on**: Ticket conditions and fare type

### Time Limits to Avoid No-Show
- **Domestic flights**: Cancel at least 2 hours before departure
- **International flights**: Cancel at least 4 hours before departure

## Refundable vs. Non-Refundable Tickets

### Refundable Tickets
- Eligible for full refund minus applicable cancellation fees
- Usually associated with Flex or higher fare classes
- More expensive at time of purchase

### Non-Refundable Tickets
- Base fare is generally not refundable
- Passengers may receive refund for:
  - Taxes
  - Airport fees
  - Subject to cancellation rules and fees

## Flight Canceled by Air India

### Passenger Rights
If Air India cancels a flight due to unforeseen circumstances:

1. **Full Refund**: Passengers are entitled to a complete refund
2. **Rebooking Option**: Free rebooking on a later flight at no additional charge
3. **Compensation**: May apply according to DGCA (Directorate General of Civil Aviation) guidelines for:
   - Significant delays
   - Flight cancellations
   - Denied boarding

### DGCA Compensation Rules
- Compensation varies based on:
  - Flight distance
  - Length of delay
  - Reason for cancellation

## Refund Process

### How to Request a Refund
1. **Online**: Use "Manage Booking" section on airindia.com
2. **Mobile App**: Air India mobile application
3. **Travel Agent**: If booked through agent, process through them
4. **Third-party Portal**: Contact original booking platform

### Refund Timeline
- **Refundable tickets**: 7-10 business days
- **Taxes/fees refunds**: May take longer (up to 30 days)
- **Credit to**: Original payment method

### Important Notes
- Refunds can be requested up to **2 years** from ticket issue date
- Processing time starts after refund request is submitted
- Bank processing may add additional days

## Special Circumstances

### Medical Emergency
- May qualify for waiver of cancellation fees
- **Required documentation**:
  - Medical certificate from registered doctor
  - Hospital admission records (if applicable)
  - Must be submitted within 7 days of cancellation

### Death in Family
- Cancellation fees may be waived
- **Required documentation**:
  - Death certificate
  - Proof of relationship
  - Submit within reasonable timeframe

### Government Travel Restrictions
- Full refund if travel is restricted by government order
- Applies to COVID-19 related restrictions and similar situations

## Tips for Passengers

### To Minimize Cancellation Fees
1. Purchase Flex fares if travel plans are uncertain
2. Cancel as early as possible (more than 72 hours before departure)
3. Use the 24-hour free cancellation window
4. Consider travel insurance for added protection

### Before Canceling
- Review your ticket's fare rules
- Check if you have travel insurance coverage
- Calculate potential fees vs. rebooking options
- Consider flight change instead of cancellation (may be cheaper)
