# Air India Flying Returns - Frequent Flyer Program

**Source**: Verified Air India Policy Data (December 2024)
**Program**: Flying Returns (Member of Star Alliance)

## Program Overview

Flying Returns is Air India's loyalty program, designed to reward frequent travelers with points, benefits, and exclusive privileges. As a Star Alliance member, Flying Returns offers global benefits across 25+ airlines.

**Program Highlights**:
- Revenue-based earning (points based on spend, not distance)
- Four membership tiers: Red, Silver, Gold, Platinum
- No blackout dates for award redemptions
- Family pooling of points
- Star Alliance benefits worldwide

## Membership Tiers

### Red (Entry Level)
**Qualification**: Automatic upon enrollment (no requirements)

**Benefits**:
- Earn Reward Points and Tier Points on Air India flights
- Earn points on Star Alliance partner flights
- Redeem points for award flights and upgrades
- 24-month point validity (evergreen with activity)

**Earning Rate**:
- 6 Reward Points per INR 100 spent
- 6 Tier Points per INR 100 spent

---

### Silver
**Qualification** (within 365 days):
- **Option 1**: 15,000 Tier Points (minimum 4,500 on Air India)
- **Option 2**: 30 flights (minimum 4 on Air India)

**Benefits**:
- All Red benefits, plus:
- **Bonus earning**: 8 Reward Points per INR 100 (33% bonus)
- Priority check-in
- Priority baggage handling
- Extra baggage allowance: +20 kg
- Star Alliance Silver status
- Access to Star Alliance lounges (when flying Star Alliance)
- Dedicated member service contact center

**Tier Validity**: 12 months from achievement

---

### Gold
**Qualification** (within 365 days):
- **Option 1**: 30,000 Tier Points (minimum 9,000 on Air India)
- **Option 2**: 60 flights (minimum 8 on Air India)

**Benefits**:
- All Silver benefits, plus:
- **Bonus earning**: 9 Reward Points per INR 100 (50% bonus)
- **Star Alliance Gold status**
- Priority boarding
- Extra baggage allowance: +20 kg (in addition to standard)
- Complimentary lounge access (Air India and Star Alliance)
- Access to 1000+ Star Alliance lounges worldwide
- Priority waitlist and standby
- Reduced change fees (approximately 50% discount)

**Tier Validity**: 12 months from achievement

---

### Platinum
**Qualification** (within 365 days):
- **Option 1**: 45,000 Tier Points (minimum 13,500 on Air India)
- **Option 2**: 90 flights (minimum 12 on Air India)

**Benefits**:
- All Gold benefits, plus:
- **Bonus earning**: 10 Reward Points per INR 100 (67% bonus)
- **Star Alliance Gold status** (highest tier)
- **Three upgrade vouchers** for domestic flights annually
- Free changes on most domestic flights
- Free cancellations on certain fare types
- Highest priority for rebooking during irregular operations
- Dedicated Platinum member service line
- Extra baggage allowance: +20 kg
- Guaranteed seat availability on select flights

**Tier Validity**: 12 months from achievement

## Earning Reward Points

### On Air India Flights

#### Base Earning (All Members)
- **6 Reward Points** per INR 100 spent (~$1.20 USD)
- **6 Tier Points** per INR 100 spent

#### Elite Bonus Earning
- **Red**: 6 points/INR 100 (base)
- **Silver**: 8 points/INR 100 (+33%)
- **Gold**: 9 points/INR 100 (+50%)
- **Platinum**: 10 points/INR 100 (+67%)

#### Direct Booking Bonus
- **+2 bonus Reward Points** per INR 100 when booking via:
  - airindia.com
  - Air India mobile app

**Example**: 
- Platinum member booking INR 10,000 ticket on airindia.com:
  - Base: 600 Reward Points
  - Platinum bonus: 400 Reward Points
  - Direct booking bonus: 200 Reward Points
  - **Total: 1,200 Reward Points**

### On Star Alliance Partner Flights

**Eligible Airlines**: United, Lufthansa, Singapore Airlines, ANA, Thai Airways, and 20+ more

**Earning**:
- **Reward Points**: Based on flight distance and fare class
- **Tier Points**: 1 Tier Point per 2 Reward Points earned
- Must provide Flying Returns number at booking

**Fare Class Multipliers**:
- First/Business: 150-200% of distance
- Premium Economy: 100-125% of distance
- Economy (full fare): 100% of distance
- Economy (discount): 25-75% of distance

### Other Earning Opportunities

#### Co-Branded Credit Cards
- **State Bank of India Air India Signature Card**:
  - 4 Reward Points per INR 100 on Air India purchases
  - 2 Reward Points per INR 100 on other purchases
  - Welcome bonus: 5,000-10,000 points

#### Hotel Partners
- Taj Hotels: 2 points per INR 100 spent
- ITC Hotels: 2 points per INR 100 spent
- Marriott Bonvoy: Transfer at varying ratios

#### Car Rentals
- Hertz: 500 points per rental
- Avis: 500 points per rental

#### Lifestyle Partners
- Dining: Select restaurants offer 1-2 points per INR 100
- Shopping: Partner retailers offer bonus points

## Redeeming Reward Points

### Award Flights

#### Air India Award Flights
**Redemption Structure** (as of 2024):
- **Variable pricing**: Points required vary based on:
  - Booking time (advance vs. last-minute)
  - Seat availability
  - Cash price of ticket
  - Route and season

**Redemption Types**:

1. **Value Fares** (Lowest Points)
   - Variable points (changes with demand)
   - Best for advance bookings
   - Limited availability
   - Example: Delhi-Mumbai from 3,000 points

2. **Prime Fares** (Fixed Points)
   - Fixed redemption rates
   - Higher points required
   - Better availability
   - Ideal for last-minute bookings
   - Example: Delhi-Mumbai from 6,000 points

#### Domestic Routes (Approximate)
- Short haul (Delhi-Mumbai): 3,000-8,000 points
- Medium haul (Delhi-Bangalore): 5,000-12,000 points
- Long haul (Delhi-Kochi): 7,000-15,000 points

#### International Routes (Approximate)
- Short haul (Mumbai-Dubai): 8,000-20,000 points
- Medium haul (Delhi-London): 25,000-60,000 points
- Long haul (Delhi-New York): 40,000-100,000 points

**No Blackout Dates**: Redeem on any available seat

#### Star Alliance Partner Awards
- Redeem on 25+ Star Alliance airlines
- Points required based on distance and cabin
- Must book through Air India

### Upgrades

**Upgrade Eligibility**:
- Must have paid ticket in lower cabin
- Upgrade by one cabin class only
- Subject to availability

**Points Required** (Approximate):
- Domestic Economy → Premium Economy: 2,000-5,000 points
- Domestic Premium Economy → Business: 3,000-8,000 points
- International Economy → Premium Economy: 5,000-15,000 points
- International Premium Economy → Business: 10,000-30,000 points
- International Business → First: 15,000-50,000 points

**Platinum Member Benefit**: 3 free upgrade vouchers annually for domestic flights

### Cash + Points

**New Feature** (2024):
- Pay for award flights using combination of:
  - Flying Returns Points
  - Cash payment
- Flexible ratio (typically 50/50 or 70/30)
- Useful when short on points

**Example**:
- Award flight requires 10,000 points
- Pay 5,000 points + INR 3,000 cash
- Or 7,000 points + INR 1,500 cash

## Point Validity and Expiration

### Standard Expiration
- **24 months** from date points are earned
- Points expire on rolling basis (oldest first)

### Evergreen Points
- Points remain valid indefinitely if:
  - You take at least **one paid Air India flight** every 24 months
  - Any earning activity resets expiration clock

### Extending Validity
- Book any paid Air India ticket
- Earn points through credit card spending
- Earn points through partner activities

## Family Pooling

### How It Works
- Combine Flying Returns Points with family members
- Reach redemption goals faster
- **No cost** to pool points

### Eligibility
- Immediate family members:
  - Spouse
  - Children
  - Parents
  - Siblings

### Process
1. Primary member initiates pooling request
2. Family members consent
3. Points can be transferred or combined for redemptions
4. Each member maintains individual tier status

## How to Join

### Enrollment
1. Visit **flyingreturns.co.in** or **airindia.com**
2. Click "Join Flying Returns"
3. Fill registration form:
   - Personal details
   - Contact information
   - Travel preferences
4. Receive membership number instantly
5. **No enrollment fee**

### Adding Number to Bookings
- Provide Flying Returns number when booking
- Add retroactively within 7 days of travel
- Ensure name matches exactly

## Tips for Maximizing Points

### Earning Strategies
1. **Book direct** on airindia.com for +2 bonus points
2. **Use co-branded credit card** for everyday spending
3. **Fly Star Alliance partners** to earn on all travel
4. **Take advantage of promotions** (double points offers)
5. **Pool with family** to reach redemption goals faster

### Redemption Strategies
1. **Book award flights early** for best Value Fare rates
2. **Use Cash+Points** when short on points
3. **Upgrade instead of booking premium** (better value)
4. **Redeem for long-haul international** (highest value)
5. **Watch for award sales** and promotions

### Status Strategies
1. **Plan tier-qualifying flights** in advance
2. **Credit all Star Alliance flights** to Flying Returns
3. **Use status benefits** (free changes, upgrades)
4. **Maintain status** with minimum activity
