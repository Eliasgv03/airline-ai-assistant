# Air India Baggage Policy

**Source**: Verified Air India Policy Data (2024-2025)

## Checked Baggage Allowance

### Domestic Flights
*   **Economy Class**:
    *   **Comfort / Standard**: 15 kg (Typical for most economy fares).
    *   **Comfort Plus / Flexi**: Up to 25 kg on select fare families.
*   **Premium Economy**: 25 kg.
*   **Business Class**: 35 kg.
*   **First Class**: 40 kg.

*Note: Star Alliance Gold members are entitled to an additional 20 kg baggage allowance.*

### International Flights
Allowances vary significantly by route (Weight Concept vs. Piece Concept).

#### General Rule (Piece Concept - e.g., to/from US, UK, Europe)
*   **Economy Class**: 2 pieces, up to 23 kg (50 lbs) each.
*   **Business Class**: 2 pieces, up to 32 kg (70 lbs) each.
*   **First Class**: 2 pieces, up to 32 kg (70 lbs) each.

#### Maximum Dimensions
*   **Economy**: Total dimensions (Length + Width + Height) must not exceed 158 cm (62 inches) per piece.
*   **Weight Limit per Bag**: No single bag can weigh more than 32 kg (70 lbs) due to health and safety regulations.

## Cabin Baggage (Hand Luggage)

### Weight Limits
*   **Economy & Premium Economy**: 1 piece up to 8 kg (approx. 17 lbs).
*   **Business & First Class**: 1 piece up to 12 kg (approx. 26 lbs).

### Dimensions
*   **max dimensions**: 55 cm x 35 cm x 25 cm (22 x 14 x 10 inches).
*   Total sum of dimensions must not exceed 115 cm (45 inches).

### Personal Item
In addition to one piece of cabin baggage, passengers are permitted to carry **one** small personal item, such as:
*   Handbag / Purse
*   Laptop bag
*   Camera bag
*   Infant bag

## Prohibited & Restricted Items
*   **Power Banks / Batteries**: MUST be carried in Cabin Baggage. Strictly prohibited in Checked Baggage.
*   **Liquids**: Containers > 100ml are not allowed in cabin baggage.
*   **E-Cigarettes**: Must be on your person/cabin bag, not checked.

## Excess Baggage
*   **Domestic**: Charged at approximately INR 600 per kg + GST.
*   **International**: Charges vary by zone. Pre-purchasing excess baggage online is recommended (up to 20% cheaper).
