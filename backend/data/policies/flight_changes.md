# Air India Flight Change and Modification Policy

**Source**: Verified Air India Policy Data (December 2024)

## 24-Hour Free Change Window

### Eligibility
- **Free changes** within 24 hours of booking
- **Condition**: Flight departure must be at least 7 days away
- **Applies to**: Both domestic and international flights
- **No modification fee** charged during this period

## Change Fees After 24 Hours

### Domestic Flights

#### Standard Change Fees
- **Economy Class**: INR 1,100 - INR 2,500
- **Premium Economy**: INR 1,000 - INR 2,000
- **Business Class**: INR 800 - INR 1,500
- **First Class**: INR 500 - INR 1,000

#### By Fare Type
- **Value Fares**: 
  - Change fee: INR 2,500
  - Plus fare difference
  - Limited flexibility
  
- **Classic Fares**:
  - More than 72 hours: INR 1,100
  - Within 72 hours: INR 2,500
  - Plus fare difference
  
- **Flex Fares**:
  - More than 72 hours: No fee
  - Within 72 hours: Minimal fee (INR 500-1,000)
  - Plus fare difference only

### International Flights

#### Standard Change Fees
- **Economy Class**: INR 2,500 - INR 5,500 (or USD 50-120)
- **Premium Economy**: INR 2,000 - INR 4,500
- **Business Class**: INR 1,500 - INR 3,500
- **First Class**: INR 1,000 - INR 2,500

#### Percentage-Based Fees
- **General rule**: 20-30% of the original fare
- **Closer to departure**: Higher percentage applies
- **Flex fares**: Lower or no fees

## How to Change Your Flight

### Online Method (Recommended)
1. Visit **airindia.com**
2. Go to **"Manage Booking"** section
3. Enter:
   - Booking reference number (PNR)
   - Last name
4. Select **"Modify Flight"**
5. Choose new flight details:
   - Date
   - Time
   - Destination (if applicable)
6. Pay applicable fees and fare difference
7. Receive confirmation email

### Other Methods
- **Call Center**: 
  - India: 1860 233 1407
  - International: +91 124 264 1407
- **Travel Agent**: If booked through agent
- **Airport Counter**: Visit Air India counter
- **Mobile App**: Air India mobile application

## Time Limits for Changes

### Domestic Flights
- **Recommended**: At least 72 hours before departure
- **Minimum**: 2 hours before scheduled departure
- **First/Business Class**: Up to 12 hours before departure

### International Flights
- **Recommended**: At least 72 hours before departure
- **Minimum**: 4 hours before scheduled departure
- **First/Business Class**: Up to 12 hours before departure

## Fare Difference

### When Fare Increases
- You must pay the difference between:
  - Original ticket price
  - New ticket price
- Plus applicable change fee

### When Fare Decreases
- **Policy varies by fare type**:
  - **Flex Fares**: May receive credit for future travel
  - **Classic Fares**: Usually no refund of difference
  - **Value Fares**: No refund of difference

## Name Changes

### Minor Corrections
- **Allowed**: Up to 3 characters
- **Examples**: 
  - Spelling corrections
  - Middle name additions
  - Title changes (Mr./Ms./Dr.)
- **Fee**: Usually INR 500-1,000
- **Required**: Government ID proof

### Complete Name Changes
- **Generally NOT allowed** except for:
  - Legal name change due to marriage
  - Legal name change due to divorce
  - Court-ordered name change
- **Required documentation**:
  - Marriage certificate
  - Divorce decree
  - Court order
  - Updated government ID

## No-Show and Post-Departure Changes

### No-Show Policy
- If you miss your flight without prior modification:
  - Ticket may become invalid
  - Only taxes may be refundable
  - Base fare typically forfeited

### Post-Departure Changes
- **Within 48 hours** of original departure:
  - May be permitted under certain conditions
  - Higher fees apply
  - Subject to seat availability
- **After 48 hours**:
  - Generally not allowed
  - Ticket may be considered expired

## Schedule Changes by Air India

### Minor Schedule Changes
- **Definition**: Less than 1 hour for domestic, less than 2 hours for international
- **Air India may**:
  - Automatically rebook you on nearest available flight
  - Notify you via email/SMS

### Major Schedule Changes
- **Definition**: 1+ hours for domestic, 2+ hours for international
- **Your options**:
  1. **Accept new schedule** (no fee)
  2. **Change to different flight** (no fee)
  3. **Full refund** (no cancellation fee)
  4. **Travel credit** for future use

### Flight Cancellation by Air India
- **Full refund** or
- **Free rebooking** on next available flight
- **Compensation** may apply per DGCA rules

## Special Fare Rules

### Group Bookings (10+ passengers)
- Different change rules apply
- Contact Air India Group Desk
- More flexibility usually available
- Separate fee structure

### Award Tickets (Flying Returns)
- Change fees in points or cash
- **Red/Silver**: Higher change fees
- **Gold/Platinum**: Reduced or waived fees
- Redeposit fees may apply

### Codeshare Flights
- If operated by partner airline:
  - Partner airline's change policy applies
  - Contact Air India for assistance
  - Fees may differ

## Tips for Changing Flights

### To Minimize Fees
1. **Book Flex fares** if plans are uncertain
2. **Change early** (more than 72 hours before departure)
3. **Use 24-hour window** for free changes
4. **Check fare difference** before confirming change
5. **Consider travel insurance** for added flexibility

### Before Making Changes
- Review your ticket's fare rules
- Check availability on desired new flight
- Calculate total cost (fee + fare difference)
- Compare with cancellation + rebooking cost
- Verify new flight times work with your schedule

## Elite Member Benefits

### Flying Returns Status Benefits

#### Silver Members
- Reduced change fees (approximately 25% discount)
- Priority rebooking during irregular operations

#### Gold Members
- Reduced change fees (approximately 50% discount)
- Free changes on certain fare types
- Priority rebooking

#### Platinum Members
- **Free changes** on most domestic flights
- Significantly reduced fees on international flights
- Highest priority for rebooking
- Three upgrade vouchers for domestic flights

## Important Notes

### Documentation
- Keep all confirmation emails
- Note your new PNR if it changes
- Update any connecting flights separately
- Inform hotel/car rental of new arrival time

### Refund Timeline
- If owed a refund due to fare decrease:
  - Credit to original payment method
  - 7-10 business days processing time
  - May be issued as travel credit instead

### Validity
- Changed tickets maintain original ticket validity
- Usually valid for 1 year from original issue date
- Some fare types may have shorter validity
