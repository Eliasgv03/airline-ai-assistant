# Air India Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Source**: Compiled from Air India Official Information (December 2024)

## Booking and Reservations

### How do I book a flight with Air India?

**Online Booking**:
1. Visit **airindia.com**
2. Select origin, destination, and dates
3. Choose your flight
4. Enter passenger details
5. Select seats (optional)
6. Add extras (meals, baggage)
7. Make payment
8. Receive confirmation via email

**Other Methods**:
- **Mobile App**: Air India mobile application
- **Call Center**: 1860 233 1407 (India), +91 124 264 1407 (International)
- **Travel Agent**: Through authorized agents
- **Airport Counter**: At Air India ticket offices

### Can I book a flight for someone else?

**Yes**, you can book for others:
- Enter their details during booking
- Payment can be made with your card
- Ensure passenger name matches their ID exactly
- Booking confirmation sent to your email
- Passenger will need booking reference for check-in

### How do I check my booking status?

**Methods**:
1. **Online**: airindia.com → Manage Booking
2. **Mobile App**: View bookings section
3. **Email**: Check confirmation email
4. **Call Center**: Provide PNR number
5. **SMS**: Text PNR to Air India number

**What You Need**:
- PNR (Booking Reference) number
- Last name of passenger

### What is a PNR?

**PNR** = Passenger Name Record
- 6-character alphanumeric code
- Unique identifier for your booking
- Example: ABC123
- Found on:
  - Confirmation email
  - Booking confirmation page
  - E-ticket
  - Boarding pass

### Can I select my seat in advance?

**Yes**, seat selection available:

**Free Seat Selection**:
- Standard seats at no charge
- Available during booking or via Manage Booking
- Subject to availability

**Paid Seat Selection**:
- **Extra legroom seats**: INR 500-2,000 (domestic), INR 1,500-5,000 (international)
- **Preferred seats**: INR 200-500 (domestic), INR 500-1,500 (international)
- Prices vary by route and seat type

**When to Select**:
- During booking (recommended)
- Via Manage Booking (up to 4 hours before departure)
- At airport check-in (subject to availability)

## Check-In

### When can I check in for my flight?

**Online/Mobile Check-In**:
- **Domestic**: 48 hours to 60 minutes before departure
- **International**: 48 hours to 75 minutes before departure

**Airport Check-In**:
- **Domestic**: 2 hours before departure
- **International**: 3 hours before departure

**Check-In Closes**:
- **Domestic**: 45 minutes before departure
- **International**: 60 minutes before departure

### How do I check in online?

**Steps**:
1. Visit **airindia.com** or use mobile app
2. Click "Web Check-In"
3. Enter PNR and last name
4. Select/confirm seats
5. Download or print boarding pass
6. If checking bags, visit bag drop counter at airport

**Requirements**:
- Valid booking
- Within check-in window
- No special assistance required (wheelchair, UM, etc.)

### Do I need to print my boarding pass?

**Options**:
- **Mobile boarding pass**: Save on phone (accepted at most airports)
- **Print at home**: Standard paper printout
- **Print at airport**: Use kiosk or counter

**International Flights**:
- Some countries require printed boarding pass
- Check destination requirements
- Recommended to have backup printout

### What if I can't check in online?

**Reasons You May Need Airport Check-In**:
- Traveling with infant
- Unaccompanied minor
- Wheelchair assistance required
- Pet in cabin
- Special meal not confirmed
- Visa/document verification needed
- Group booking

**Solution**: Visit airport check-in counter

## Baggage

### How much baggage can I bring?

**See our detailed Baggage Policy document for complete information.**

**Quick Reference - Domestic**:
- Economy: 15-25 kg checked + 8 kg cabin
- Business: 35 kg checked + 12 kg cabin

**Quick Reference - International**:
- Economy: 2 pieces × 23 kg + 8 kg cabin
- Business: 2 pieces × 32 kg + 12 kg cabin

### What items are prohibited in baggage?

**Never Pack in Checked Baggage**:
- Power banks and spare batteries
- E-cigarettes and vaping devices
- Lighters (more than one)
- Flammable liquids
- Explosives and fireworks

**Never Carry in Cabin Baggage**:
- Sharp objects (scissors, knives)
- Liquids over 100ml (unless in checked bag)
- Tools and sporting equipment

**Completely Prohibited**:
- Weapons and firearms (unless authorized)
- Illegal drugs and substances
- Hazardous materials

### What if my baggage is lost or damaged?

**Immediate Action**:
1. Report to Air India baggage counter **before leaving airport**
2. File PIR (Property Irregularity Report)
3. Keep PIR reference number
4. Provide contact details

**Follow-Up**:
- Track status online with PIR number
- Air India will contact you when found
- Delivery to your address (usually free)

**Compensation**:
- **Delayed baggage**: Interim expenses may be reimbursed
- **Lost baggage**: Compensation up to SDR 1,131 (~USD 1,500) per passenger
- **Damaged baggage**: Repair or compensation based on assessment

**Timeline**:
- Report damage within 7 days
- Report delay within 21 days
- Lost baggage claim within 21 days

## Flight Changes and Cancellations

### Can I change my flight date or time?

**Yes**, flight changes are allowed:
- **Within 24 hours of booking**: Free (if departure is 7+ days away)
- **After 24 hours**: Change fee applies + fare difference

**Change Fees**:
- Domestic: INR 1,100-2,500
- International: INR 2,500-5,500
- Varies by fare type and timing

**See our Flight Changes Policy document for details.**

### How do I cancel my flight?

**Methods**:
1. **Online**: airindia.com → Manage Booking → Cancel
2. **Mobile App**: Cancel booking option
3. **Call Center**: 1860 233 1407
4. **Travel Agent**: If booked through agent

**Refund Timeline**:
- 7-10 business days to original payment method
- May take up to 30 days for international bookings

**See our Cancellation Policy document for fees and details.**

### What if Air India cancels my flight?

**Your Rights**:
1. **Full refund** (no cancellation fee)
2. **Free rebooking** on next available flight
3. **Compensation** (per DGCA rules, if applicable)

**Notification**:
- Air India will contact you via email/SMS
- Provide alternative flight options
- Refund processed automatically if not rebooked

## At the Airport

### When should I arrive at the airport?

**Recommended Arrival Times**:
- **Domestic flights**: 2 hours before departure
- **International flights**: 3 hours before departure
- **Peak travel times**: Add 30-60 minutes
- **Special assistance needed**: Add 30 minutes

### What documents do I need?

**Domestic Flights** (India):
- Government-issued photo ID:
  - Passport
  - Aadhaar card
  - Voter ID
  - Driving license
  - PAN card
- Boarding pass (print or mobile)

**International Flights**:
- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Visa (if required for destination)
- Boarding pass
- Return/onward ticket (if required)
- COVID-19 documents (if required)
- Health declarations (if required)

**For Children**:
- Birth certificate or passport
- School ID (for domestic)

### Can I use airport lounges?

**Lounge Access**:

**Complimentary Access**:
- Business/First class passengers
- Flying Returns Gold/Platinum members
- Star Alliance Gold members

**Paid Access**:
- Purchase day pass at lounge entrance
- Prices vary by airport (typically INR 1,500-3,000)

**Credit Card Access**:
- Many premium credit cards offer lounge access
- Check your card benefits

**Lounges**:
- Maharaja Lounge (Air India exclusive)
- Star Alliance lounges
- Partner lounges

## In-Flight Experience

### What meals are served on Air India flights?

**Domestic Flights**:
- **Short haul** (under 90 min): Snacks and beverages
- **Medium/long haul**: Full meal service
- Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options

**International Flights**:
- Multi-course meals
- Choice of vegetarian/non-vegetarian
- Special meals available (pre-order required)
- Complimentary beverages including alcohol

**Special Meals**:
- Must be requested 24 hours in advance
- Options: Vegan, Jain, Diabetic, Gluten-free, etc.
- See our Special Services document

### Is there in-flight entertainment?

**Availability**:
- **Wide-body aircraft**: Seat-back screens with movies, TV, music, games
- **Narrow-body aircraft**: Streaming to personal devices (select flights)
- **Domestic short-haul**: Limited or no IFE

**Content**:
- Bollywood and Hollywood movies
- International TV shows
- Music albums
- Games
- Moving map

**Wi-Fi**:
- Available on select aircraft
- Paid service (pricing varies)
- Free messaging on some flights

### Can I use my phone during the flight?

**Airplane Mode Required**:
- Must be in airplane mode during flight
- Wi-Fi can be enabled if available
- Bluetooth allowed for headphones

**When to Turn Off**:
- During takeoff and landing
- When crew announces

**Allowed Devices**:
- Phones, tablets, laptops
- E-readers
- Portable chargers (under 100Wh)

## Flying Returns (Loyalty Program)

### How do I join Flying Returns?

**Enrollment**:
1. Visit **flyingreturns.co.in**
2. Click "Join Now"
3. Fill registration form
4. Receive membership number instantly
5. **Free to join**

**Benefits**:
- Earn points on Air India and Star Alliance flights
- Redeem for award flights and upgrades
- Tier benefits (Silver, Gold, Platinum)

### How do I earn points?

**On Air India Flights**:
- 6 points per INR 100 spent (base)
- Elite members earn bonus points
- +2 bonus points when booking on airindia.com

**Other Ways**:
- Star Alliance partner flights
- Co-branded credit cards
- Hotel stays
- Car rentals

**See our Flying Returns document for complete details.**

### How do I redeem points?

**Redemption Options**:
1. **Award flights** on Air India and Star Alliance
2. **Upgrades** to higher cabin class
3. **Cash + Points** combination

**How to Redeem**:
- Login to flyingreturns.co.in
- Search for award flights
- Book using points
- Pay taxes and fees

## Special Situations

### Can I travel if I'm pregnant?

**Yes**, with restrictions:
- **Up to 32 weeks** (domestic): No restrictions
- **Up to 36 weeks** (international): No restrictions
- **32-36 weeks** (domestic): Medical certificate required
- **After 36 weeks**: Not permitted to travel

**Medical Certificate Must State**:
- Expected delivery date
- Fitness to fly
- No complications
- Issued within 7 days of travel

### Can I travel with my pet?

**Yes**, pets allowed:

**In-Cabin** (small pets):
- Dogs and cats under 5 kg (including carrier)
- Fee: INR 2,000-3,000 (domestic), INR 5,000-10,000 (international)
- Must book 48 hours in advance

**Checked** (larger pets):
- In cargo hold
- IATA-approved carrier required
- Charged as excess baggage

**Requirements**:
- Health certificate
- Vaccination records
- Import/export permits (international)

**See our Special Services document for details.**

### What if I need wheelchair assistance?

**Wheelchair Service**:
- Available at no charge
- Must request at booking or 48 hours before
- Types: WCHR, WCHS, WCHC

**Includes**:
- Assistance through airport
- Priority boarding
- Help to/from seat
- Escort to baggage claim

**How to Request**:
- During booking
- Via Manage Booking
- Call center: 1860 233 1407
- Email: specialassistance@airindia.in

## Refunds and Compensation

### How long does a refund take?

**Timeline**:
- **Refundable tickets**: 7-10 business days
- **Cancellation refunds**: 7-10 business days
- **International bookings**: Up to 30 days
- **Credit card refunds**: Plus 5-7 days for bank processing

**Refund Method**:
- Original payment method
- Same card/account used for booking

### What compensation am I entitled to for delays?

**DGCA Rules** (India):

**Domestic Flights**:
- **2-4 hour delay**: Meals and refreshments
- **4+ hour delay**: Meals + hotel (if overnight)
- **Flight cancellation**: Full refund or alternate flight

**International Flights**:
- Varies by country and route
- EU flights: EU261 compensation may apply
- US flights: DOT rules apply

**How to Claim**:
- Keep all receipts
- File claim with Air India customer service
- Provide flight details and documentation

## Miscellaneous

### Can I request a specific meal?

**Yes**, special meals available:
- Must request at least 24 hours before departure
- Options: Vegetarian, Vegan, Jain, Diabetic, Gluten-free, etc.
- Request via Manage Booking or call center

### Is Wi-Fi available on flights?

**Availability**:
- Select wide-body aircraft
- Primarily on international routes
- Check during booking or with crew

**Pricing**:
- Varies by flight and duration
- Typically USD 8-20 for full flight
- Free messaging on some flights

### Can I bring duty-free items on board?

**Yes**, duty-free allowed:
- Must be in sealed tamper-evident bag
- With receipt showing purchase within 48 hours
- Counts toward cabin baggage allowance
- Liquids over 100ml allowed if duty-free

### What if I left something on the plane?

**Lost and Found**:
1. **Report immediately**: Contact Air India at arrival airport
2. **File report**: Provide flight details and item description
3. **Check online**: Some airports have online lost and found
4. **Follow up**: Call airport lost and found department

**Contact**:
- Airport lost and found: Specific to each airport
- Air India customer service: 1860 233 1407
- Email: support@airindia.in

**Timeline**:
- Items held for 30-90 days
- Valuable items may be held longer
- Shipping available (at your cost)
