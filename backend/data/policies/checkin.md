# Air India Check-in Rules

**Source**: Verified Air India Policy Data (2024-2025)

## Check-in Windows

### Web / Mobile Check-in
*   **Domestic Flights**: Opens **48 hours** prior and closes **1 hour** (60 mins) before scheduled departure.
*   **International Flights**: Opens **48 hours** prior and closes **2 hours** (120 mins) before scheduled departure.

### Airport Counter Check-in
*   **Domestic Flights**: Counters close **60 minutes** before departure.
*   **International Flights**: Counters close **75 minutes** before departure.

*Recommendation: Arrive at airport 3 hours prior for International and 2 hours prior for Domestic flights.*

## Boarding Process
*   **Gate Closure**: Boarding gates close strictly **20 minutes** prior to departure for all flights.
*   **Document Verification**:
    *   **Domestic**: Valid Photo ID (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID, Driving License, or Passport).
    *   **International**: Valid Passport (min 6 months validity) + Visa (if applicable).

## How to Check-in Online
1.  Visit `airindia.com` or the Air India Mobile App.
2.  Navigate to "Manage Booking" or "Check-in".
3.  Enter your **13-digit E-ticket Number** or **Booking Reference (PNR)** and Last Name.
4.  Select seats and download/print your Boarding Pass.
    *   *Note: For some international sectors, you may receive a "Confirmation Slip" instead of a boarding pass; you must exchange this at the airport counter for document verification.*

## Baggage Drop
*   If you have checked baggage, proceed to the "Baggage Drop" or dedicated "Web Checked-in" counters at the airport.
*   Bags must be dropped before the counter closure time (60 mins Domestic / 75 mins International).

## Automated Check-in
For select flights, Air India may automatically check you in and email the boarding pass 12 hours prior to departure if you haven't done so yourself, assigning an available seat automatically.
